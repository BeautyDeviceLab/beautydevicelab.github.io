How to choose an LED face mask (routine-first)

Pick a routine you’ll keep

LED isn’t a one-night miracle. The win is a schedule that doesn’t feel like work.

Comfort matters

If it’s bulky or annoying, usage drops. Comfort and fit often beat “more modes.”

Simple vs multi-colour

Multi-colour masks are great to start. Premium devices can feel more convenient and “finished.”

Canada buying checklist

Check seller, returns, warranty support, and what’s included (power supply, straps, manuals).

FAQ

How often should I use an LED mask?

Most people do best with short, consistent sessions across multiple weeks. Follow your device’s instructions and keep it comfortable enough to repeat.

Do multi-colour masks matter?

They can help you start, but consistency and comfort usually matter more than extra modes. The best device is the one you keep using.

Can I combine LED with IPL or microcurrent?

Many people combine tools. Go slow, follow manufacturer guidance, and separate sessions if your skin gets irritated. When in doubt, keep it simple.

Is LED safe?

Always follow instructions and safety notes. If you have a medical condition or are under treatment, consult a qualified professional.
